---
title: Things That Aren't Doing the Thing
category: Inspirational
tags: [Inspirational]
star: true
---

Preparing to do the thing isn't <b>doing the thing</b>.

Scheduling time to do the thing isn't <b>doing the thing</b>.

Making a to-do list for the thing isn't <b>doing the thing</b>.

Telling people you're going to do the thing isn't <b>doing the thing</b>.

Messaging friends who may or may not be <b>doing the thing</b> isn't <b>doing the thing</b>.

Writing a banger tweet about how you're going to do the thing isn't <b>doing the thing</b>.

Hating on yourself for not <b>doing the thing</b> isn't <b>doing the thing</b>. Hating on other people who have done the thing isn't <b>doing the thing</b>. Hating on the obstacles in the way of <b>doing the thing</b> isn't <b>doing the thing</b>.

Fantasizing about all of the adoration you'll receive once you do the thing isn't <b>doing the thing</b>.

Reading about how to do the thing isn't <b>doing the thing</b>. Reading about how other people did the thing isn't <b>doing the thing</b>. Reading this essay isn't <b>doing the thing</b>.

The only thing that is <b>doing the thing</b> is <b>doing the thing</b>.
